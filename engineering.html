How It Works

The Process

Every project follows the same disciplined path — from your first message to the finished product in your hands.

01
Consultation

You bring the problem or idea. We ask the right questions and define what needs to be built.

02
CAD Design

We model the solution in 3D, producing technical drawings and geometry ready for fabrication.

03
Prototype

A working prototype is built and tested. You review, we refine until it's right.

04
Fabrication

Full production using the right process — 3D printing, metal fab, CNC, or a combination.

05
Delivery

Finished, inspected, and delivered. The idea is now a physical reality.
